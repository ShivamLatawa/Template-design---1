About La Spa De Vita

Here at La Spa De Vita we pride ourselves on delivering upscale spa treatments and services for your individual needs. We invite you to come relax, unwind, and enjoy our unique tranquil day spa setting. We are located in downtown Lytle's Historic District off of Adams Street.
We work with different lines of 100% organic products that deliver results you can see and feel.
